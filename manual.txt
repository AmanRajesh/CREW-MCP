Symptom: blinking amber SPO2 sensor
Diagnosis: The internal LED element or the photodiode in the SPO2 connector has degraded beyond the safe calibration threshold.
Resolution: Replace the entire SPO2 sensor assembly.
Part Number (SKU): SPO2-AMB
Safety Precautions: 
- CAUTION: Ensure the patient monitor is powered down completely before disconnecting the sensor. 
- WARNING: Do not test the new sensor on yourself; use the standardized calibration phantom block.
---
Symptom: Error Code 404B
Diagnosis: Display backlight inverter failure.
Resolution: Replace inverter board.
Part Number (SKU): 404B
Safety Precautions:
- DANGER: High voltage capacitors on the inverter board may retain charge. Wait 5 minutes after power down.
